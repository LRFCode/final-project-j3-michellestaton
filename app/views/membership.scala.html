Welcome to NLBC

We affirm the Bible as the inspired Word of God, the basis for our belief. It is the written Word and Christ is the living Word. We subscribe to the doctrinal statement of THE BAPTIST FAITH AND MESSAGE as adopted by the Southern Baptist Convention in June 2000. We want to share the good news of Jesus Christ with unbelievers. By providing meaningful worship and Bible studies, we lead church members to participate in personal and family growth experiences.

Our service times:

Sunday worship at 8:15 a.m. and 10:45 a.m.

Life Groups meet Sunday morning at 9:30 a.m.

Wednesday Bible Study begins at 6:30 p.m.
